We are CHRONOFUND

PLAY VIDEO
Chronofund is a unique project with no similar alternative on the global market. We offer a completely new model of profiting from watch resale with minimum risk.

Our clients can buy watches below market price with the opportunity to earn on reselling them through Ineichen Zurich – one of the leading watch auction houses in Switzerland.

Originally, we offered inclusive service of watch resale for Ineichen Zurich’s clients. When penetrating new regions such as Japan, Russia, and the USA, we created strong partnerships, which allowed us to buy exceptional timepieces below the market price.

However, taking this opportunity to the full required substantial capital – available only through borrowing. As the result, we thought of a project, where capital can be created by others in exchange for this earning opportunity and Ineichen Zurich’s expertise accumulated since 1973.

“We seek resources, you seek new earning opportunities. Chronofund is to allow both of us get what we want and create common profits.”
Artem Lechbinckiy
CEO of Ineichen Zurich
