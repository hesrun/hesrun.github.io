ABOUT ARTLE

Founded by a group of watch experts and connoisseurs in association with the leading Swiss watch auctioneer, ARTLE gives an exclusive gathering of discerning watch collectors from all over the world.

And we’re not talking any watches, we’re talking the crème de la crème of the world of horology. Dedicated to share our passion for Haute Horlogerie, we strive to stock selected, truly special timepieces that represent investment value for the enthusiast, collector and a man of impeccable taste alike.

ARTLE customers will have an exclusive access to our bespoke selection of rarest, highly sought-after pieces created by the most important historic brands and independent watchmakers and members of the The Académie Horlogère des Créateurs Indépendants.

Since its establishment in 1973, Ineichen Auctioneers have been one of the leading names among Swiss auctioneers. A name that stands for reliability, knowledge and quality. The solid base to select and trade rare timepieces.

Each timepiece from our selection is carefully inspected by our in-house experts from Ineichen Auctioneers to ensure that its provenance and authenticity are of the highest standard.

Discover carefully handpicked icons by the grand maisons like Patek Philippe, A. Lange & Söhne, Vacheron Constantin, Audemars Piguet, Breguet, Jaeger-LeCoultre and Ulysse Nardin, and uniqueness of one-off creations by the independent watchmakers, such as Svend Andersen, Konstantin Chaykin, Beat Haldimann, Vianney Halter, Francois Paul Journe, Daniel Roth and Kari Voutilainen.
