Our Collectors Office

The ARTLE Collectors Office was founded by a watch connoisseurs and collector with support of the Auction House Ineichen - one of the swiss leading watch auctioneer since 1973.

It is our goal to provide collectors with all services they need. We consult our clients if they want to buy a timepiece for their collection or to sell one. We estimate and find out the market price of our clients' timepieces to make it profitable for them. We repair and overhaul watches if there is a need and help to bring them on auctions.

We help our clients to find rare and antique timepieces manufactured by Patek Philippe, A. Lange & Söhne, Vacheron Constantin, Rolex, IWC, Audemars Piguet, Breguet, Jaeger-LeCoultre and Ulysse Nardin.
