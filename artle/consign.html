We offer our clients
a sale-on-commission service
called consignment.

Working with our in-house experts from the leading Swiss auction house Ineichen Zürich, we will take possession of your watch, advertise it though our channels and find its next owner.

Our fee varies from 10% to 15% depending on the piece’s value and negotiation. The higher the value of your watch, the lower is our percentage commission.

Contact us to discuss the specifics of your timepiece and gather the information to properly market it, including condition and provenance.
