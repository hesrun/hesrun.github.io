Flexible participation options

100% PREPAYMENT
We pay 100% Purchase price when the watch is delivered to office
You earn:
Purchase price
If the watch is not sold - the watch belongs to us

*We promise not to sell under the agreed Reserve price not to spoil the market reputation of the watchmaker
50% PREPAYMENT
We pay 50% of the Purchase price
when the watch is delivered to the office

We pay another 50% of the Purchase price
when the watch is sold

Purchase price is then withdrawn from the Hammer price

(Hammer price - Reserve price) is shared 50/50
You earn:
Purchase price + 0,5(Hammer Price - Reserve price)
If the watch is not sold - we buy the watch out (Conditions discussed individually)
COMMISSION
No prepayment for the watch.
Сommission depends on the Hammer price:

Hammer < CHF 50 000 • 20%
CHF 50 000 ⋜ Hammer ⋜ CHF 150 000 • 15%
Hammer > CHF 150 000 • 15%
You earn:
Hammer price - Commission
If the watch is not sold - returned to the watchmaker
